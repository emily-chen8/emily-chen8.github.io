Academics

Second year

Major: Data Science and Biochemistry

Minor: Mathematics

Hi, my major stated above seems rather daunting (probably because it is) and the way I got to it is actually pretty simple. In high school I was a "science nerd" (and I was proud so it's ok). I performed research in lab setting essentially every summer and loved it. As I applied for colleges people said I should look into computer science at least as a minor and then I saw the combined major of data science and biochemistry and went for it. As for the math minor, I looked at my academic plan and saw the only math I was required to take was statistics and bioinformatics and I absolutely love math and calculus and could not even stand the thought of not taking it in college... I'd just miss it too much :)
